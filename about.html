About Us

Family-Owned Since 1979

Welcome to Economy Floors, your trusted source for quality flooring and countertop solutions in Nephi, Utah. Founded in 1979, we’ve proudly served Central Utah homeowners and contractors with unbeatable service, products, and prices for over four decades.


Meet the Owners

We are Stephanie and Shane Woolslayer, proud owners of Economy Floors. As a family-run business, we take pride in offering personal, one-on-one service and making sure every project—big or small—is handled with care and precision. Our customers aren’t just clients—they’re neighbors and friends.


What we Offer

At Economy Floors, we specialize in:

  • Carpet – Comfortable, stylish options for every room.
  • Tile – Durable and decorative, perfect for kitchens, bathrooms, and beyond.
  • Hardwood Flooring – Timeless beauty and strength.
  • Granite Countertops – Elegant, long-lasting stone for kitchens and baths.
  • Installation Services – Professionally installed by our experienced team.
  • Supplies – Tools and materials for DIYers and contractors alike.

Our Commitment

We believe in honest pricing, quality workmanship, and building lasting relationships. After all these years, we’re still committed to the same values we started with: Integrity. Quality. Community.
